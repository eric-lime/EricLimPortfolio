Student Analysis

Majors in demand!

Project Link

PDF Version

For this project, I decided to explore two datasets of first year students at Whitman College over the years. The dataset conatins information regarding all the first year students' major, terms, courses taken, and gpa.

With this data, I was able to join the two datasets on the key of the student IDs and generated different types of comparisons/visualization through the use of matplotlib and numpy. For example, the pie chart above shows a common major, Chemisty, and what percentage of those students are associated with what race in order to see the spread of diversity within one of the most common majors.

This project helped me learn a lot more about how dataframes work and the differences in join methods. I also realized the significance of comparisons and its methods when it comes to working with columns. Through the comparisons, I was able to use my plot skills in order to create visualizations that clearly depict the results. Overall, a very fun and insightful project about diversity/majors and their progression!
