This is a hi-fidelity prototype of a website called “Board Games at Distance” that I made for my Human-Computer Interaction course through Adobe XD and Invision. The idea around the design is enabling the connection between board game lovers throughout the social distancing restrictions of Covid.

This was a project that I really enjoyed as it was my first time going through the iterative cycle of design(Observation, Idea Generation, Prototyping, Testing) in order to produce a final hi-fidelity product.

It was within this project where I realized how significant critical feedback is within design as it helped me get a better sense of users' thought processes when interacting with the design by retracing their steps, reminding them of their actions, and questioning them.
