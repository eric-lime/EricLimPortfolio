Roomie Radar!

Find your next roommate(s)!

Project Link

Here is a mobile app design project that I designed through Figma within my free time. It’s called Roomie Radar! Think of it as Tinder but for finding roommates and meetups for users who are trying to relocate somewhere else but are having a hard time finding a roommate to live with.

I enjoyed this project a lot because it’s a bit more advanced than my previous projects and I learned a lot of new helpful features within Figma that I didn’t know existed before such as auto-layouts and masks!

My favorite part of the project was making the meetups page as this was where I found out how useful masks are when dealing with images and frames. It also helps the design look a lot cleaner :)
